Reflection Logging

Turn daily observations into lasting change through consistent, honest reflection.

Every morning at 5:30am, you capture three types of entries:

Story Mining — What happened yesterday worth keeping?

Reflection — What pattern showed up? What did I learn?

Commitment — What will I be careful about today?

Over time, patterns emerge. Clarity compounds. Growth happens.

Your Daily Practice (5:30 AM)

A structured 30-minute morning reflection ritual

2 minutes
Centering
  • Sit quietly
  • Deep breaths
  • Set intention: "What can I learn today?"
5 minutes
Story Mining
  • What happened yesterday worth keeping?
  • Scan for incidents/conversations
  • Log story entry via form
5 minutes
Quick Pattern Scan
  • Open section-tree.md
  • Circle 1-2 sections that showed up
15 minutes
Deep Diagnosis
  • Log Reflection entry
  • Log Commitment entry
  • Use NVC framework
3 minutes
Closure
  • Read the daily quote
  • Gratitude for one insight
  • Close and move forward
Total
30 Minutes
